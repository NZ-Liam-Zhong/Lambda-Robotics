Principal AI Research Engineer - RL

Engineering•SF•Full time•On-site

Lambda Robotics builds autonomous systems for physical infrastructure. As a Principal AI Research Engineer focused on reinforcement learning, you'll lead the development of policies that let our robots act autonomously in unstructured, real-world environments.

What you'll do

  • Design and train RL policies for physical control and decision-making on real hardware.
  • Build the simulation-to-real pipelines that get policies from training to deployment.
  • Set technical direction for the RL research effort and mentor other engineers.
  • Close the loop between research results and what actually works in the field.

What we're looking for

  • Deep experience training and deploying RL policies, ideally on physical systems.
  • Track record of taking research from idea to working system, not just papers.
  • Comfort with the realities of sim-to-real transfer and hardware constraints.
  • Ability to set direction independently at a principal level.
