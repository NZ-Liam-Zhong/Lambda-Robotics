Controls Engineer

Engineering / SF / Full time

Lambda Robotics builds autonomous systems for physical infrastructure. As a Controls Engineer, you'll design and implement the feedback loops that let our hardware operate reliably in unstructured, real-world environments, not simulations.

What you'll do

  • Design, model, and tune control systems for autonomous mobile platforms.
  • Develop state estimation and sensor fusion pipelines running on embedded hardware.
  • Work directly with mechanical and electrical engineering to close the loop from hardware to software.
  • Debug control behavior in the field, not just on the bench.

What we're looking for

  • Strong fundamentals in classical and modern control theory.
  • Experience with real-time embedded systems (C/C++) and ROS or similar middleware.
  • Comfort working with hardware-in-the-loop and physical prototypes, not just simulation.
  • Bias toward shipping and iterating in the field.
