How It Works

Deploy Once.
Runs Every Day After That.

Think of it like hiring a permanent staff member — except they never call in sick, never quit, and cost a fraction of local wages.

01

You Tell Us What You Need

Pick the type of job, your location, and how many hours a day you need covered. We'll put together a custom annual quote within 24 hours.

📋
02

We Install the Robot at Your Property

Within 48 hours of signing, our team comes to your site, installs the robot, and configures it for your space. No construction. No permits. You just let us in.

🤖
03

A Dedicated Remote Worker Is Assigned to You

We assign a trained worker — based in Southeast Asia or Latin America — to your account. They operate your robot on your schedule, every day. If they're unavailable, a backup covers automatically.

🌏
04

Work Happens. One Annual Fee. Done.

One flat annual invoice. No overtime. No benefits. No surprise costs. The robot stays on-site, the work gets done every day, and you get monthly reports on task completion.
